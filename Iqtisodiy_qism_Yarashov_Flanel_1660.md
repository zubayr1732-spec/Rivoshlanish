#### Hisoblashlar:

**1. To'quv dastgohining nazariy ish unumdorlik normasi hisobi (formula 57):**

$$A = \frac{n_{bv} \times 60}{P_a \times 10} = \frac{400 \times 60}{140 \times 10} = \frac{24000}{1400} = \mathbf{17{,}143 \text{ m/soat}}$$

bu yerda:
- $n_{bv}$ — bosh valning aylanish soni = 400 ayl/min
- $P_a$ — arqoq zichligi = 140 ip/dm
- 10 — dm dan m ga o'tkazish koeffitsienti

**2. Foydali vaqt koeffitsienti hisobi (formula 58):**

$$FVK = K_a \times K_b = 0{,}93 \times 0{,}85 = \mathbf{0{,}7905}$$

bu yerda:
- $K_a$ = 0,93 — "a" guruhi to'xtashlari koeffitsienti (tanda va arqoq uzilishlari)
- $K_b$ = 0,85 — "b" guruhi to'xtashlari koeffitsienti (texnik xizmat, g'altak almashtirish)

**3. Dastgohning haqiqiy mahsulot ishlab chiqarish normasi aniqlanadi:**

$$H_1 = A \times FVK = 17{,}143 \times 0{,}7905 = \mathbf{13{,}551 \text{ m/soat}}$$

$$H_2 = H_1 \times B_{xt} = 13{,}551 \times 1{,}694 = \mathbf{22{,}956 \text{ m}^2/\text{soat}}$$

$$H_3 = H_1 \times P_a \times 10 = 13{,}551 \times 140 \times 10 = \mathbf{18\,971 \text{ arqoq/soat}}$$

$$H_4 = H_3 \times B_{tig} = 18\,971 \times 1{,}8393 = \mathbf{34\,893 \text{ m.arq/soat}}$$

## V. MEHNAT BO'YICHA REJALASHTIRILGAN TEXNIK-IQTISODIY KO'RSATKICHLAR HISOBI

**1. Mehnat unumdorligi (m/ishchi-soat) (formula 59):**

$$\Pi_m = \frac{B_m}{O_{ch.ch}} = \frac{1\,881\,560}{48 \times 7{,}41 \times 2 \times 280} = \frac{1\,881\,560}{199\,180{,}8} = \mathbf{9{,}45 \text{ m/ishchi-soat}}$$

bu yerda: $B_m$ = 1 881 560 m (21-jadval, 19-ustun); $O_{ch.ch}$ = 48 × 4 149,6 = 199 180,8 ishchi-soat

**2. Mehnat unumdorligi (arqoq/ishchi-soat) (formula 61):**

$$\Pi_{arq} = \frac{B_{arq}}{O_{ch.ch}} = \frac{2\,634{,}10 \times 10^6}{199\,180{,}8} = \mathbf{13\,224 \text{ arqoq/ishchi-soat}}$$

**3. Ro'yxatdagi ishchilar soni:**

$$Ch_{sp} = 48 \text{ kishi}$$

**4. 100 ta dastgoh uchun ishchi sarfi (formula 62):**

$$\Pi_{100} = \frac{Ch_{sp}}{n_{dastgoh}} \times 100 = \frac{48}{35} \times 100 = \mathbf{137{,}14 \text{ kishi/100 dastgoh}}$$

**5. O'rtacha soatlik ish haqi:**

$$\bar{Z}_{soat} = \frac{J_1 \times 1000}{O_{ch.ch}} = \frac{988\,258{,}6 \times 1000}{199\,180{,}8} = \mathbf{4\,963{,}0 \text{ so'm/soat}}$$

**6. O'rtacha kunlik ish haqi:**

$$\bar{Z}_{kun} = \frac{J_2 \times 1000}{Ch_{ya} \times K_{rd}} = \frac{1\,003\,082{,}4 \times 1000}{48 \times 280} = \frac{1\,003\,082\,400}{13\,440} = \mathbf{74\,634 \text{ so'm/kun}}$$

bu yerda: ishlagan kishi-kunlari = 48 × 280 = 13 440

**7. O'rtacha oylik ish haqi (formula 63):**

$$\bar{Z}_{oy} = \frac{J_3 \times 1000}{Ch_{sp} \times 12} = \frac{1\,093\,359{,}8 \times 1000}{48 \times 12} = \frac{1\,093\,359\,800}{576} = \mathbf{1\,898\,194 \text{ so'm/oy}}$$

## VI. TO'QUVCHILIKDA MAHSULOT TANNARXI HISOBI

Rejalashtirilgan mahsulotni ishlab chiqarish bilan bog'liq bo'lgan tannarxni, ya'ni harajatlarni puldagi umumiy yig'indisini aniqlash bitiruv ishining muhim bo'limlaridan biri hisoblanadi. Mahsulot tayyorlashda ishlab chiqarishdagi umumiy harajatlar summasini aniqlashda faqat to'qima ishlab chiqarish bilan bog'liq harajatlarni hisobga olish kerak.

Mahsulot ishlab chiqarish tannarxi harajatlari quyidagi guruhlardan iborat:
1. Moddiy ishlab chiqarish harajatlari
